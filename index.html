HANBIN'S COMFORT ZONE

Comfort Zone

  • Staying quiet in the class, even if I think I have something useful to contribute to the class.
  • Talking in person, rather than to large crowd.
  • Rather stay quiet than be wrong mentality.
  • Keep things to myself, even though it feels like something is wrong.
  • Leave the talk to a group member that is more comfortable with it, even though I may have better knowledge of certain subject matter.
  • Staying in my inner circle than trying to connect with more people.

Where the success is made.

  • Talking in front of people.
  • Actively engaging in class participation
  • Engaging in healthy dialogue.
  • Try to provide constructive feedback to teammate or during class.
  • Practice talking in front of people with the group and slowly build up confidence in talking to larger crowd.
  • Actively networking with people in my field from class, volunteer, and career fair.
